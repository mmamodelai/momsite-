About

Helping people feel heard, supported, and capable

I'm a Certified Peer Support Specialist through the State of California, with a lifetime of helping people through stressful, high-stakes moments. I believe deeply in meeting people where they are — with patience, honesty, and zero judgment — and walking alongside them until they find their footing.

Before peer support, I spent decades as a California real estate broker, guiding families through some of the biggest decisions of their lives. The heart of that work was never really the property — it was listening, building trust, and helping people stay steady when everything felt overwhelming. That's the same gift I bring to peer support today.
