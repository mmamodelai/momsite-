✓✓ CONFIRMED: her cert IS the Medi-Cal Peer Support Specialist Certification

  • Verified in the official CalMHSA public registry: MPSS-GJSHEL · Charlene Marie Clare · San Diego · Issued May 16, 2025 · Expires May 16, 2027 · Status: ACTIVE. CalMHSA is the State of California's certifying entity for the Medi-Cal Peer Support Specialist Certification — there is no separate cert to chase. The "MPSS" in her number stands for Medi-Cal Peer Support Specialist.
  • Employers can verify it themselves: app.capeercertification.org/cert/MPSS-GJSHEL — this link is now on the resumes too. County and Medi-Cal-funded roles love this.
  • One date to remember: the cert renews every two years — renewal (with continuing-education hours) is due before May 16, 2027.
